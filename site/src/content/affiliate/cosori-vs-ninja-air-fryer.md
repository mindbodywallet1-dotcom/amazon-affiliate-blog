Both fryers cook well. The decision comes down to how much counter space you can give up and how many people you cook for.

## Cosori Lite overview

The Cosori Lite is built for minimal footprint. At 2.1 quarts, it handles single servings and couple-sized meals without dominating the counter.

**Strengths:** Smallest footprint, simplest controls, easy cleanup.

**Weaknesses:** Small basket limits batch cooking.

## Ninja AF101 overview

The Ninja AF101 steps up to 4 quarts while staying reasonably compact. It is the better pick when you regularly cook for three or four.

**Strengths:** More capacity, versatile temperature range, strong reviews.

**Weaknesses:** Larger footprint and slightly louder operation.

## Which should you buy?

| Priority | Pick |
|----------|------|
| Smallest possible size | Cosori Lite |
| Cooking for 3–4 people | Ninja AF101 |
| Simplest interface | Cosori Lite |
| Most versatile capacity | Ninja AF101 |

## Verdict

There is no universal winner. Tight studio kitchen → Cosori Lite. Small family or shared apartment → Ninja AF101.
