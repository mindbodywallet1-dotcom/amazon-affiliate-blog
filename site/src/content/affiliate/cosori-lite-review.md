The Cosori Lite targets a specific buyer: someone who wants air-fried results without dedicating half the counter to an appliance.

## Who is this for?

Apartment dwellers, dorm residents, and couples who cook 1–2 servings at a time. If you batch-cook for a family, the 2.1-quart basket will feel cramped.

## Key features

- 2.1-quart capacity
- Single-dial time and temperature control
- Dishwasher-safe nonstick basket
- Compact footprint for small kitchens

## What we like

Setup takes minutes. The dial interface means no app pairing or menu diving. For frozen fries, wings, or reheating leftovers, results are consistently crisp.

## What to consider

Capacity is the trade-off. A full frozen pizza will not fit. Cooking for three or more means working in batches.

## Verdict

If counter space is your bottleneck, the Cosori Lite delivers. It is not the most powerful or largest air fryer — it is the one that fits where others will not.
