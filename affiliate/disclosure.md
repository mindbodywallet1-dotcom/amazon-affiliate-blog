Use this text across all Amazon affiliate landing pages. The short version
goes in the disclosure banner; the full version can appear in the footer.

This page contains affiliate links to Amazon.com. If you click a link and
make a purchase, I may earn a commission at no additional cost to you. I
only recommend products I have researched and believe offer genuine value
based on features, reviews, and use cases.

Prices and availability on Amazon change frequently. Always verify current
pricing on Amazon before purchasing.

As an Amazon Associate I earn from qualifying purchases.

## Placement rules

1. Short disclosure must appear above the fold on every affiliate page.
2. Full disclosure must appear in the page footer.
3. Social posts linking to affiliate pages must include #ad or "affiliate link"
   where platform rules require it.
